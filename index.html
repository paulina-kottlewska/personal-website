My Favorite Books

BY BUS AROUND THE WORLD
by Karol and Aleksandra Lewandowscy

The books describe the authors' trips, their experiences, tips, and thoughts on traveling. They can be used as a guide for planning a trip to the places mentioned in the titles.

  • Ameryka za 8 dolarów (USA for 8 dollars)

THE WITCHER SERIES
by Andrzej Sapkowski

The Witcher is a series of five fantasy novels and short stories (collected in two books) about the witcher Geralt of Rivia. The witcher is a beast hunter with supernatural abilities. The beasts that the witcher encounters during his journey are based on the myths and legends that the author heard about during his trips.

  1. Ostatnie życzenie (The Last Wish)
  2. Miecz przeznaczenia (The Sword of Destiny)
  3. Krew elfów (Blood of Elves)
  4. Czas pogardy (Time of Contempt)
  5. Chrzest Ognia (Baptism of Fire)
  6. Wieża jaskółki (The Tower of the Swallow)
  7. Pani Jeziora (The Lady of the Lake)
  8. Sezon burz (Season of Storms)

PEŁNA MOC ŻYCIA.
JEŚLI O CZYMŚ W ŻYCIU MARZYSZ - SIĘGNIJ PO TO.
(Full Power of Life -
If you dream of something - make it come true)
by Jacek Walkiewicz

The author of the book is a psychologist, speaker, coach, and mentor. In this book, he writes about the experience he gained through over twenty years of practice. He shares his insights about dreams, life, change, values, and courage through funny and thought-provoking stories.

THE HAPPIEST MAN ON EARTH:
THE BEAUTIFUL LIFE OF
AN AUSCHWITZ SURVIVOR
by Eddie Jaku

Eddie Jaku always considered himself a German first and a Jew second. He was proud of his country. But all of that changed in November 1938 when he was beaten, arrested, and taken to a concentration camp. Over the next seven years, Eddie faced unimaginable horrors every day, first in Buchenwald, then in Auschwitz, and finally on a Nazi death march. He lost family, friends, and his country. Because he survived, Eddie made a vow to smile every day. He pays tribute to those who were lost by telling his story, sharing his wisdom, and living his best possible life.

BEZ OGRANICZEŃ.
JAK RZĄDZI NAMI MÓZG.
(Without Limits.
How The Brain Governs Us)
by Jerzy Vetulani i Maria Mazurek

The book is in the form of a conversation between an outstanding neurobiologist and a journalist. Jerzy Vetulani, in a simple, funny, and sometimes controversial way, talks about the mysteries of a complex and still not fully discovered organ: the human brain.
